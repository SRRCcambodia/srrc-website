Our Implementation Projects

FTTH Network Design & Deployment

SRRC has designed and implemented over 2500 GPON sites across Cambodia. Our scope includes full-cycle fiber network design, backbone planning, distribution network layout and onsite deployment supervision.

BTS Infrastructure Deployment

More than 1500 BTS sites deployed from 2021–2025, including tower construction, civil works, equipment installation, antenna configuration and network integration.

Large Building Network Systems

Comprehensive internet and structured cabling systems for major buildings, including government and commercial complexes. Projects include Ministry-level buildings and KYI Center.

Inbuilding Solutions (IBS)

Design and deployment of indoor telecom coverage systems including distributed antenna systems (DAS), signal optimization and indoor fiber architecture.

Underground & Aerial Fiber Projects

Execution of large-scale fiber optic deployment projects, including underground trenching, aerial fiber installation, splicing, testing and commissioning.

Integrated Telecom & Civil Works

Combined technical and construction capability delivering complete telecom infrastructure solutions from planning to operation.
